Honeybee

Bacnotan goes as far as adopting a healthier lifestyle and finding an alternative livelihood in honey production. So the government decide to established a honeybee center. The center serves as a facility for processing raw honey, as well as an equipment store for those who want to start beekeeping. Honey byproducts produced in the center include honey vinegar, massage oil, all-purpose balm, honey and propolis (substance collected by bees from leaf buds and tree barks) soap and bee pollen.

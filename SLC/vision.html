We, the Saint Louis College of the City of San Fernando, La Union,

a collaborative Missionary community

commit to the integral human formation of the Youth

to become Christ-centered and competent leaders

responsive to the needs of Church and society

by providing quality and transformative instruction,

animating campus ministry, engaging in relevant researches

and sustainable extension programs, forging ties

with partner agencies, and fostering justice,

peace, and integrity of creation.
